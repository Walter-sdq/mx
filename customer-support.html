Frequently Asked Questions

How do I deposit funds?

You can deposit funds through various methods including bank transfer, credit card, and cryptocurrency. Visit the Deposits section in your dashboard to get started.

How long do withdrawals take?

Withdrawal processing times vary by method: Bank transfers (1-3 business days), Cryptocurrency (10-60 minutes), Credit cards (instant to 24 hours).

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email address, and follow the instructions sent to your email.

What are the trading fees?

We offer competitive spreads starting from 0.1 pips. No commissions on standard accounts. Check our pricing page for detailed fee structure.
